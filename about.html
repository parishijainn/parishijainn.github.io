About Me

I’m a first-generation American with two younger twin brothers. My journey is shaped by family, culture, and a passion for teaching and accessibility. Here are some parts of my story:

Family

Being the oldest sibling has taught me leadership and responsibility. My family motivates me to advocate for inclusive education and technology.

Dance

I’ve been dancing Indian classical since I was 4 years old. Dance keeps me grounded in tradition while also letting me express creativity and discipline.

Teaching & Mentorship

In high school, I taught robotics to middle schoolers, including underprivileged students. I’m passionate about bridging the gap between tech and opportunity.

Robotics

As a member of my VEX Robotics team, I loved building and competing with robots. These experiences deepened my interest in engineering and problem-solving.
